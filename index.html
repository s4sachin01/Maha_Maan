About Us

Powered by Trust, Driven by Quality – Since 1997

Established in 1997, Mahamaan Motors is an authorized distributor of Maruti Genuine Parts (MGP) in Haryana, serving MASS, dealers, workshops, and customers with reliability and trust.

Mr. Ashok Sharma

Chief Executive Officer | B.Sc. (Electronics)

With over 30 years of experience in the automobile trade, Mr. Ashok Sharma has been the driving force behind the group’s growth, innovation, and long-term success.

His expertise spans marketing, financial management, and strategic leadership, ensuring strong systems, efficient operations, and sustainable expansion.
